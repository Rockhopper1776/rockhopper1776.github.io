RandomBot Buff Module

mod-rndbot-buffs is an AzerothCore module for mod-playerbots that lets idle random bots cast useful class buffs on nearby real player characters.

The module is designed conservatively: bots only participate when they are alive, unmounted, out of combat, not casting, and above a configurable mana threshold. It respects existing buffs and only casts at most one buff per check.

  • Priests can cast Fortitude, Divine Spirit, and Shadow Protection.
  • Mages can cast Arcane Intellect.
  • Druids can cast Mark of the Wild and Thorns.
  • Paladins can cast Blessing of Kings, Wisdom, or Might depending on the target.
