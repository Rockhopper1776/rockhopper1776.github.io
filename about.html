About

About Rockhopper 1776

Rockhopper 1776 is my personal workshop for historical writing, tabletop-gaming tools, and hobbyist software projects.

About me

I am a U.S. Marine Corps veteran, military historian, hobbyist developer, and lifelong wargamer. I hold an M.A. in military history, and my interests sit at the intersection of history, strategy, technology, and game design. I enjoy both tabletop and computer wargames, especially those that bring military history to life through thoughtful systems and meaningful decisions.

About the site

This is a place for finished work as well as projects in progress: historical essays, tools that make games easier to play, AzerothCore experiments, and notes worth keeping. The site is intentionally straightforward so the work remains the focus.
