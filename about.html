About Rockhopper 1776

Rockhopper 1776 is my personal site for collecting historical writing, tabletop-gaming tools, and hobbyist software projects.

About me

I am a U.S. Marine Corps veteran, military historian, hobbyist developer, and lifelong wargamer. I hold an M.A. in military history, and my interests sit at the intersection of history, strategy, technology, and game design. I enjoy both tabletop and computer wargames, especially those that bring military history to life through thoughtful systems and meaningful decisions. This site is a place for my projects, experiments, notes, and reflections on the games, tools, and historical subjects that interest me.

This site is intentionally simple: a place to organize projects, notes, and writing without turning the website itself into a complicated project.
